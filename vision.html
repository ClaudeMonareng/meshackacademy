Our Vision

  • To be a leader in Maths, Science and Digital Technology Tutoring in the KwaZulu-Natal region, by offering innovative and world-class curriculum planning, management and delivery.

Our Mission

  • Offer high quality multi-media content delivery in small classes of no more than 15 learners per session.
  • Activity Based Learning
  • Frequent Marking and Performance Feedback.
  • Prioritise use of latest online learning material.
  • Facilitate the franchise of the Academy to more branches in and around Amajuba District in the first five years of its first inception.

Our Value Statements

  • The best interest of the child and then the parent - this principle governs all our work.
  • Student Centeredness - provide students centered services that take cognisance of the rights of young people.
  • Students Participation - ensures children's voices are listened to and are heard in all of our work.
  • Accountability - remain accountable to the parents we serve and our partners.
  • Transparency - remain transparent in all aspects.
  • Respect - demonstrate respect to all person of all classes, race, language, religion, culture and sexual orientation.
  • Communication - maintain open and direct communication.
  • Representation - reflect all South African cultures in our management team, staff and volunteers.
  • Development and Empowerment - efficient and effective staff, volunteers, organizational structures, policies and procedures that will enhance service delivery to students.
